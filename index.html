Ping Pong!

Let's play!

Rules:

Enter a number, and the webpage will count up to that number (starting from 1) and display those numbers in a list with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become 'ping'
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become 'pong'
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become 'pingpong'
